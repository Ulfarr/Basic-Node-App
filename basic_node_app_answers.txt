Exercises

1. What is a Node module?

A: Node modules are pieces of code that contain everything needed to execute a certain function. They are portable, encapsulated and reusable. 

2. What is the main difference between exports and module.exports?

A: module.exports can be reassigned to anything while exports can’t  be reassigned outside the module.

3. Why is using exports recommended?

A: It gives more flexibility due to not being restricted to a specific object type.
